About Robo Cor

My name is Robert Cordina, also known as Robo Cor.

My journey in music began at the age of 14, when I learned to play guitar and started writing my own songs. Music quickly became more than a hobby — it became a way to express thoughts, emotions and life experiences.

During my youth, I played in a local music band, performing original songs and developing my own musical style. Although life later took me in different directions, music always remained an important part of who I am.

Today, modern technology and AI-powered tools allow me to continue that journey in a completely new way. They help me transform ideas into songs, stories into music, and emotions into visual experiences.

Robo Cor is where past and future meet — human creativity, life experience and new technology working together in harmony.

Welcome to my world of music.
